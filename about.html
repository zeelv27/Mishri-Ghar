ABOUT

Mishri Ghar is the home of Indian sweets, bringing the rich flavors of traditional mithai to life. Known for its authentic recipes and high-quality ingredients, Mishri Ghar offers a wide variety of sweets, from classic favorites like gulab jamun, rasgulla, and kaju katli to regional specialties such as Mysore pak and Peda. Delivering a warm and inviting atmosphere, Mishri Ghar captures the essence of Indian hospitality, ensuring every bite is a taste of nostalgia and joy.

ORIGIN

The name “Mishri Ghar,” meaning “House of Sugar Crystals,” reflects its commitment to crafting sweets that are not only delicious but also a symbol of love, celebration, and togetherness. Established decades ago, Mishri Ghar quickly gained popularity for its purity, taste, and quality.
