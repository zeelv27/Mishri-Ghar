Dry Desserts

 Basundi

 Basundi is a sweetened condensed milk   made by boiling the milk on low untl it   is reduced by half.

 Cham Cham

 Cham Cham is panner (cottage chesse)  shaped in cylindrical dumplings and then  boiled sugar syrup.

 

 Barfi

 Barfi is a dense, milk-based fudge made  with condensed milk, sugar, and ghee  (calrified butter) and has multiple  flavours.

  • Anjeer
  • Besan
  • Chocolate
  • Coconut
  • Kaju
  • Malai
  • Mango
  • Pista 
  • Rose 

 Dhoodpak

 Dhoodpak is a sort of rice pudding  made from milk, rice, sugar, safforn and  nuts. 

 Gulab Jamun

 Gulab Jamun is deep fried dough balls  made from khoya (reduced milk) or milk  powder and soaked is sugar syrup.

Ghari

 Ghari is a dessert made from khoya  (reduced milk), ghee (calrified butter) and  suagr with a nutty filling and shaped into  a round dumping. 

 Falooda

 Falooda is a cold dessert with  vermicelli, flavoured syrup, basil seeds, jelly  and ice cream which is often served  with milk.

  • Kesar 
  • Rabri
  • Rose
  • Tutty Fruity

 Jalebi

 Jalebi is a crispy sprial shaped dessert  made by deep frying fermented batter and  soaked in sugar syrup.     

 Gujia

 Gujia is a deep-fried, crescent-shaped  pastry filled with a sweet mixture of  khoya, sugar, and nuts. 

 Fruit Custard

 Fruit Custard is a blend of custard and  condesed milk with a mix of chopped  fresh fruits.     

 Kala Jamun 

 Kala Jamun is similar to Gulab Jamun but  it is fried for longer and has nuts stuffed in  the mdiddle.

 Halwa

 Halwa is a dessert made by cooking ghee  (calrified butter), sugar and milk with  either semonila, carrota, lentils depending  on the flavour.

  • Badam
  • Gajar
  • Mango
  • Moong
  • Pineapple
  • Sooji 

 Kheer

 Kheer is a rice pudding with milk, sugar  and cardamom and garnished off with  nuts.

 Kulfi

 Kulfi is a dense, creamy Indian ice cream  made from thickened milk, sugar with  multiple flavors.

  • Almond
  • Butterscotch
  • Cassata
  • Chocolate
  • Kesar
  • Malai
  • Mango
  • Pista
  • Rose
  • Strawberry

 Ice Halwa

 Ice Halwa is a thin translucent sheet  dessert made from milk, sugar and ghee  (calrified butter), garnished with safforn  and nuts.    

 Rabri

 Rabri is a rich condensed milk dessert  which is boiled until it's thick and  layered with malai (cream).  

 Rasgulla

 Rasgulla is a soft, spongy dessert made  from paneer (cottage cheese) balls cooked  in light sugar syrup.

 Kaju Katli

 Kaju Katli is a smooth, diamond-shaped  cashew fudge made from cashew nuts,  sugar, and ghee (calrified butter).

 Rasmalai

 Rasmalai is soft panner (cottagge  chesse) dumplings soaked in sweetened  safforn infused milk.

 Shrikhand

 Shrikhand is a creamy, sweetened yogurt  dessert flavored with cardamom, saffron  and nuts.

 Kalakand

 Kalakand is a milk-based sweet made by  reducing milk and sugar, resulting in a  grainy, moist texture. 
   

 Kopra Pak

 Kopra Pak is a coconut-based sweet made  with desiccated coconut, sugar, and milk  solids.
   

 Ladoo

 Ladoo is round, bite-sized sweets made  from flour, sugar, and ghee (calrified  butter), available in different varieties. 

  • Besan
  • Boondi
  • Churma
  • Coconut
  • Motichoor
  • Wheat 
   

 Lapsi

 Lapsi is made from broken wheat (dalia)  cooked with ghee and jaggery.
   

 Malpua

 Malpua is deep fried pancakes made from  flour, milk and sugar which served with a  sugar syrup.
   

 Milk Peda

 Milk peda is thick, milk-based sweet  made by reducing milk and sugar, often  flavored with cardamom.
   

 Modak

 Modak is a dumpling-shaped sweet filled  with jaggery and coconut.
   

 Mohantal

 Mohantal is made with besan (gram flour)  fudge and ghee (calrified butter) which is  infused with cardamom.
   

 Mysore Pak

 Mysore Pak is made with gram flour,  large amounts of ghee (calrified butter)  with a melt in the mouth texture.
   

 Petha

 Petha is a translucent chewy dessert made  from winter melon and sugar syrup.
   

 Seviyan

 Seviyan is a vermicelli-based dessert  cooked with milk and sugar, topped with  nuts and saffron.
   

 Shahi Tukda

 Shahi Tukda is a bread pudding where the  bread is fried then soaked in thickened  milk with cardamom, safforn and nuts.
   

 Soan Papdi

 Soan Papdi is a flaky and crispy dessert  made from gram flour, ghee (calrified  butter), sugar and cardamom.
   

 Sukhdi

 Sukhdi is made from whole wheat flour,  ghee (calrified butter) and jaggery with a  soft dense texture.
